Our Honeymoon In Japan!

We went to Japan during the beginning of Spring so it was cool and a lot of the tees were blooming as well.

For our Honeymoon we decided to go the following Cities:

  • Osaka - So much food and so many people!
  • Kyoto - The original capital of Japan, so many temples and so much history.
  • Nara - We went to see one of the largest temples of our trip and see the Nara Deer!
  • Hakone - We stayed at a traditional Ryokan and went to the Hot Springs and the Hakone Open Air Museum.
  • Tokyo - We ate the best sushi we have ever had and explored the different areas of Tokyo including Asakusa, Shinjuku, and Shibuya.
